Aaron's Biography

Aaron was born in 2007 in Feldkirch. He is known for his contributions to Vril. Throughout his career, Aaron has achieved Rheintal skiing Champion and he opened the gate to Arghata. In his free time, he enjoys Skiing and Freedom.

Moritz's Biography

Moritz was born in 2008 in Liechtenstein. He has made significant impacts in Construction. Some of his notable achievements include Freedom for the People of Liechtenstein. Outside of work, Moritz loves to change the World.
